Frequently Asked Questions

How does FreshPlate work?
Simply add ingredients to your virtual fridge with expiration dates. FreshPlate automatically suggests recipes based on what you have and prioritizes items about to expire.
Is FreshPlate really free?
Yes, 100% free! No hidden costs, no subscription, no credit card needed. Your data stays on your device.
Do I need to create an account?
No signup required! FreshPlate works entirely in your browser. Just open and start using it immediately.
How do I earn points?
Earn points by: Adding ingredients (+5), Uploading photos (+10), Using items before expiry (+10-15), Cooking recipes (+20).
