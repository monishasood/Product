The Problem

Most food waste is preventable

You buy it. You forget it. It expires. FreshPlate breaks that cycle with smart tracking and AI-powered suggestions.

  • Forgetting expiry dates is the #1 cause of avoidable food waste at home
  • Most people don't know what recipes they can make with what they already have
  • Out of sight, out of mind — items at the back of the fridge go to waste unnoticed

FAQ

Good
questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

Add ingredients to your virtual fridge with names, quantities, and expiry dates. FreshPlate tracks urgency automatically and, when you enable AI Recipes, sends your ingredient list to Claude AI to generate personalized recipe suggestions tailored to exactly what you have.

Yes — 100% free with no account required. The base app works entirely in your browser. The AI recipe feature uses the Anthropic API; you'll need your own API key (available free from Anthropic) to enable it.

Everything stays in your browser's localStorage — no servers, no accounts, no data sharing. Clear your browser data and it resets. Your API key is only ever used client-side and never transmitted to FreshPlate.

The AI feature sends your current ingredient list to Claude (Anthropic's AI) which generates 3–5 creative, practical recipe ideas customized to your exact inventory — including difficulty, cook time, and descriptions. It's far better than a static recipe database.

Absolutely. FreshPlate is fully responsive and works on all modern browsers — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge — on desktop and mobile.
